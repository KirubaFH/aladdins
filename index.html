about us

At Aladdin’s, we have built our multiple award-winning brand on a passion for great food, genuine care, and a commitment to serving every community with quality and consistency. What started as a single store has grown into a trusted, design-led takeaway brand that reflects our dedication to flavour, freshness, and dependable service. Every step of our journey is guided by the promise to deliver a memorable experience, one meal at a time.

From freshly prepared pizzas to crowd-favourite kebabs and grills, Aladdin's is committed to delivering flavour, convenience, and a smooth ordering experience from start to finish ensuring customers receive reliable service, day after day. Whether you are collecting or ordering for delivery, you can always count on us for quality and speed.
