FOOD ALLERGY NOTICE

Please note that all of our food is produced in a kitchen that handles food products containing allergens including, peanuts, tree nuts, sesame, fish, egg, shellfish (molluscs & crustaceans), milk, cereals containing gluten, celery/celeriac, soya, mustard and sulphites.

Although precautions are taken in consideration to avoid cross contamination, it is impossible to fully guarantee complete separation of these allergens at all times.

WE DO NOT CATER FOR ALLERGENS OR INDIVIDUALS WITH ALLERGIES AND RESERVE THE RIGHT TO REFUSE SERVICE AT OUR DISCRETION.

Before ordering, please speak to our staff members about any allergies or special dietary requirements.
